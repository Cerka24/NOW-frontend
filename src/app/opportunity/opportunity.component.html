Short description

We are seeking dedicated individuals interested in making a meaningful impact on political research through volunteer work. As a Political Research Assistant, you will play a vital role in supporting our team's efforts to gather, analyze, and synthesize data related to political trends, policies, and electoral campaigns. This position offers an excellent opportunity to gain firsthand experience in the field of politics, contribute to informed decision-making processes, and actively participate in shaping the political landscape.

Requirements

Conduct Research: Assist in conducting comprehensive research on political topics, including policy issues, legislative proposals, voting patterns, and political candidates. Utilize various reliable sources such as academic papers, reputable news outlets, and official government publications to gather relevant information. Data Collection and Analysis: Collect, organize, and analyze data pertaining to political campaigns, public opinion polls, and demographic trends. Help create detailed reports, charts, and presentations to effectively communicate research findings to team members and stakeholders. Monitor Political Events: Stay up to date with local, national, and international political events, including elections, debates, and policy discussions. Summarize key developments, analyze their potential implications, and provide regular updates to the research team. Assist with Fieldwork: Support fieldwork activities such as conducting surveys, interviews, and focus groups.
